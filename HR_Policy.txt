ABC Technologies - Human Resources Policy

Working Hours:
Employees are required to work from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday.

Attendance:
Employees must maintain a minimum attendance of 95%.

Leave Policy:
Employees are entitled to 20 paid annual leaves and 10 sick leaves per year.

Work From Home:
Employees may work remotely for up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Probation Period:
New employees will undergo a probation period of 6 months.

Employee Conduct:
All employees are expected to maintain professional behavior and follow company ethics.
