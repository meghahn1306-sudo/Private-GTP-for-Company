ABC Technologies - IT Security Policy

Password Requirements:
Passwords must contain at least 8 characters including uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

Password Change:
Employees must change their passwords every 90 days.

VPN Usage:
Employees working remotely must connect through the company VPN.

Software Installation:
Only software approved by the IT department may be installed on company devices.

Data Security:
Sharing confidential company data with unauthorized individuals is strictly prohibited.

Email Usage:
Official email accounts should be used only for work-related communication.
