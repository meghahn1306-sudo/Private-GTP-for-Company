ABC Technologies - Leave Management Policy

Annual Leave:
Employees are entitled to 20 annual leave days.

Sick Leave:
Employees are entitled to 10 sick leave days annually.

Maternity Leave:
Eligible employees may receive up to 26 weeks of maternity leave.

Paternity Leave:
Eligible employees may receive up to 10 days of paternity leave.

Leave Approval:
All leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal and approved by the reporting manager.

Unused Leave:
Unused annual leave may be carried forward up to a maximum of 10 days.
